Code for Good

Currently I am enrolled to a program and contest called code for good that is being hosted by JPMorgan. Code for good is a hackathon that allows other coders to collagborate with each other in order to develop innovative technology solutions for nonprofit organizations. It will also help me start learning about a technology career with JPMorgan.

I got involved with this due to the sheer amount of good that this brings to so many people. Organizaitons that are smaller may not have access to enough capital in order to purchase vital software for the growth of their business. This type of competition helps bridge that gap and really form good connections between other smaller or larger companies.
